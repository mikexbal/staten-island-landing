Visit Staten Island

Enjoy Staten Island’s culture, parks, and sports—all just a short ride from Manhattan.

Top activites to do in Staten Island

Ride the Staten Island Ferry

Enjoy a scenic ride on the Staten Island Ferry, with stunning views of the NYC skyline and Statue of Liberty.

Go for a hike

Take on a refreshing hike through the Staten Island Greenbelt’s trails and forests.

Visit the Botancical Garden

Explore the beautiful Snug Harbor Botanical Garden, a peaceful oasis full of plants and flowers.
